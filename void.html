This browser is not for everyone.
It is not for regular people,
It is not for multitaskers,
But, it may be for you.

keyboard shortcuts:

  • Ctrl+Alt+S - Show Address Bar
  • Ctrl+R - Reload
  • Ctrl+Left - Back
  • Ctrl+Right - Forward
  • Escape - Hide Address Bar
  • Ctrl+Q - Quit
  • Ctrl+H - Go Home
  • Ctrl+Shift+S - Toggle Darkmode
